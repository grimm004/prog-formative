How Is Computing Used In These Cases

Medical Informatics is defined as the discipline which is the study, invention and implementation of structures and algorithms to improve communication, understanding and managment of medical information. This is a multidiscplinary field but the main aim is to promote new technolgies which will improve the standard of healthcare around the globe.

Health Informatics tackles the problem of access to healthcare information. It attempts to optimise the acquisition of information in the fields of healthcare and biomedicine. This discipline makes use of IT applications to develop algorithms which collect, store and analyse data in digitial formats.

Biomedical Informatics is the science of information as applied to or studied in the context of patho-physiological processes with methods from biology, chemistry and physics. This discipline pursues the effective uses of biomedical data, information, and knowledge for scientific inquiry, problem solving, and decision making, motivated by efforts to improve human health.

Pharmacy Informatics is the field field that focuses on medication-related data and knowledge within the field of healthcare systems impacting on patient care and management. This discipline has created inventions such as Intelligent pills that can deliver targeted drug therapy to specific areas of the body, monitor the bodyâ€™s environment (e.g. pH levels, temperature), and otherwise support informatics efforts by transmitting data via wireless technology. Other pharmacy technologies like mobile apps, sensors on medication caps, and skin patches can also gather patient information send it out to their care team via the cloud.

Cheminformatics requires use of computer and informational techniques, applied to a range of problems in the field of chemistry; more relevantly in the drug discovery process in the healthcare field. Chemoinformatics is the mixing of those information resources to transform data into information and information into knowledge for the intended purpose of making better decisions faster in the area of drug lead identification and optimization. Cheminformatics combines the scientific working fields of chemistry, computer science and information science for example in the areas of topology, chemical graph theory, information retrieval and data mining in the chemical space. Cheminformatics can also be applied to data analysis for various industries like paper and pulp, dyes and such allied industries.
